Core concept

What is incrementality?

Incrementality uses gold standard experimental methodology to determine which ads are driving additional conversions that wouldn't have otherwise taken place.

Attribution asks "who was there?"
Incrementality asks "who caused it?"

It's the difference between media that works and media that just shows up.

Without incrementality measurement, you cannot tell which conversions your ads actually caused.

Risk assessment

Incrementality testing relies on experience

Getting it wrong doesn't just waste budget — it trains your models wrong and locks in bad decisions.

Media is more complex than ever

  • Audience overlap and codependency skew results
  • AI ad targeting introduces systematic bias
  • Contaminated control groups destroy accuracy
  • No API monitoring = costly, undetected errors

Risk tolerance leaves little room for error

  • Holdout tests >33% can compromise revenue
  • Micro-holdouts or short tests give false confidence
  • Rushing speed over value wastes spend

"Easy" tools lead to costly mistakes

  • Random market selection can't extrapolate to national performance
  • Synthetic controls without simulation-based design produce unreliable baselines
  • No in-flight API monitoring means errors compound undetected
  • Training MMM on flawed experimental priors locks in bad decisions
